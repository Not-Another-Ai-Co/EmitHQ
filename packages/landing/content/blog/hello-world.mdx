We're launching the EmitHQ blog. This is where we'll share what we're learning about webhook infrastructure — the technical decisions, the architecture trade-offs, and the lessons from building a reliable delivery platform.

## What to Expect

We'll be writing about:

- **Architecture decisions** — why we chose persist-before-enqueue, how we handle retry with jitter, and what our circuit breaker looks like in practice
- **Pricing and market analysis** — the $49-$490 gap in webhook infrastructure and why it exists
- **Open-source transparency** — build-in-public updates, metrics, and honest takes on what's working and what isn't

## Why a Blog?

Developer tools live and die by technical credibility. Blog posts let us show our work — not just what EmitHQ does, but _how_ it does it and _why_ we made the choices we did.

If you're evaluating webhook infrastructure or building your own delivery pipeline, we hope you'll find something useful here.
